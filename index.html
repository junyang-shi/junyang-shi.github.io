Biography

  • Email: jshi28 AT binghamton.edu

Worked as a member of the Ansys Electronics Business Unit (EBU) R&D team. Solid background in different programming languages (C/C++/Python) and research assistant in the department of computer science at the State University of New York at Binghamton. I received a B.S. degree from Huazhong University of Science of Technology (HUST). My interests include the Internet of Things (IoT), algorithms, simulation, embedded systems, cyber-physical systems, and wireless sensor networks. I am a student member of IEEE.
