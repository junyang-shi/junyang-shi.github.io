Biography

I am a Ph.D. student in the Department of Computer Science at Binghamton University (State University of New York at Binghamton) under the guidance of Dr. Mo Sha. I received a B.Eng. degree from Huazhong University of Science of Technology. My research interests include Industrial Wireless Sensor-Actuator Networks, Internet of Things, and Embedded Systems.
