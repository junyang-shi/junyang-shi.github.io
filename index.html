Biography

I am a Ph.D. candidate in the Department of Computer Science at Binghamton University (State University of New York at Binghamton) under the guidance of Dr. Mo Sha. I received a B.S. degree in Electrical Engineering from Huazhong University of Science of Technology (HUST). My research interests include Industrial Wireless Sensor-Actuator Networks, Internet of Things, and Embedded Systems.
